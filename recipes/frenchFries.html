French Fries

Ingredients (Serves 2)

  • 2 large Russet potatoes
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into sticks (about 1/4 inch thick).
  2. Place the potato sticks in a bowl of cold water for at least 30 minutes to remove starch (this makes them crispy!).
  3. Drain the potatoes and pat them completely dry with paper towels. (Water causes hot oil to splatter, be careful!).
  4. Heat about 2 inches of oil in a deep pan or pot to 350°F (175°C).
  5. Carefully add the potatoes in batches. Do not overcrowd the pan.
  6. Fry for about 5-6 minutes until they are soft and pale (not brown yet). Remove and drain on a rack.
  7. Increase the oil heat slightly. Return the potatoes to the oil and fry for another 2-3 minutes until golden brown and crispy.
  8. Drain on paper towels and sprinkle with salt immediately while hot.
